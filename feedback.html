Swim Feedback

Help tune the swim tide ratings

What to send

The app gets tide times from a nearby tide station. The bit that needs local help is the swim rating: how the tide height feels for getting in, swimming near shore, and getting out.

Send the beach, date, time, water feel, and your 0-5 tide rating. A short note or photo helps a lot.

Send it back to me in a message. If you have this app, you probably already know where to find me.

Star guide

0 starsToo shallow or awkward for a shore swim.
1-2 starsSwimmable only with a long wade or awkward entry/exit.
3 starsUsable, but not the best tide for depth or access.
4 starsGood swim tide with comfortable near-shore depth.
5 starsBest tide window around high water.
